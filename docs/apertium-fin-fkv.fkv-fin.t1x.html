    Macros

    Macros are helper functions in apertium transfer files.

    default-conversion1

    Parametres: 1
    1. let tl[1]['a_known'] ≔ ""
    2. let tl[1]['x_func'] ≔ ""
    3. let tl[1]['a_func'] ≔ ""
    4. let tl[1]['a_dialect'] ≔ ""

    Rules

    The actual rules concerning stuff.

    REGLA: Necessive

    Matching pattern:

    1. pron-pers
    2. necessive-verb

    Action:

    1. default-conversion1($1)
    2. default-conversion1($2)
    3. Output:
      1. [
        1. tl[1]['lem'] <prn.pers> tl[1]['a_pers'] tl[1]['a_nbr'] <gen>
        2. b0
        3. tl[2]['lem'] tl[2]['a_verb'] tl[2]['tense'] <p3.sg>
        ]necessive <necessive>
